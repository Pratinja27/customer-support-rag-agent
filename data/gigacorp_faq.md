# Return Policy

Customers may return products within **30 days** of delivery.

Products must:

- Be unused
- Include original packaging
- Include accessories
- Include proof of purchase

---

## Damaged Products

If a product arrives damaged:

1. Contact customer support within 48 hours.
2. Upload photos of the damage.
3. Our support team will review the request.
4. A replacement or refund will be offered after verification.

---

## Exchange Policy

Customers can request product exchanges within 30 days.

Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

---

# Refund Policy

Approved refunds are processed within **5–7 business days**.

Refunds are returned using the original payment method.

Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the error was caused by GigaCorp.

---

## Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued for:

- Missing accessories
- Minor customer-caused damage
- Open-box returns

---

# Warranty

All GigaCorp products include a **1-year limited warranty**.

Premium members receive an extended **2-year warranty** on eligible products.

Warranty covers:

- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware failures
- Battery defects

Warranty does NOT cover:

- Water damage
- Physical damage
- Unauthorized repairs
- Normal wear and tear

---

## Warranty Claim Process

1. Submit a warranty request.
2. Provide order number.
3. Upload photos or videos.
4. Technician reviews the request.
5. Replacement or repair is scheduled.
